Usage rules

  • Keep clearspace around the mark equal to the height of the arrow stem.
  • Use the cream A and mint 4 on dark, ink, or warm off-white backgrounds.
  • Do not rotate, outline, recolor into purple, or place inside circular badges.
  • For tiny contexts, use the mark without the word Action402.
